Troubleshooting

Why do my uploads keep failing?

  • The size of the snippet or image you are trying to upload is exceeding Imgur's max upload file size.
  • You may have exceeded Imgur's max uploads per hour. Imgur allows a max of 50 uploads every hour.
  • Imgur may be down for maintenance.
  • Make sure you are using the latest version of Snipping Tool++
