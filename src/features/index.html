What features does Snipping Tool++ have?

  • Upload to Imgur
    By using a reliable and fast image hosting provider such as Imgur you can rest easy knowing your uploads will be there. Because we use Imgur, you can easily delete any of your uploads from their servers.
  • Upload via FTP
    Coming Soon!

    Want your images saved to your own personal FTP server? Soon you will be able to!
  • Multi-Image Uploader
    Quickly upload an entire album of images to Imgur and have a list of links ready in a matter of seconds.




  • Saves locally
    Backups of all your uploads are saved locally on your computer. Don't want to upload your image? Thats fine, Snipping Tool++ allows you to choose whether your image is uploaded or saved locally only.
  • Image Editor
    Your snippet or screenshot is automatically sent to our basic editor where you can add text, draw lines, blur or block out sensitive information before uploading to Imgur.
  • Capture Viewer
    The capture viewer allows you to quickly browse any snippet or screenshot that you either saved or upload and provides you with options to open, send to the image editor, re-upload, or delete from Imgur.
  • Customizeable Preferences
    Change the data directories to a new location, choose whether you want the Image Editor enabled and more.
  • Great Support!
    Having problems with Snipping Tool++? Send us an email at contact@snippingtoolpluspl.us. We will personally help you resolve any issue you are having with Snipping Tool++, we try to respond as soon as we get your email.
